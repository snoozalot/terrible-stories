I Write Terrible Stories

“I’m sorry I told you my name.”
There’s no record of us here. Just static and a receipt with no ink.

🦌

You’re inside. I’m outside. The waiting room smells like denial.
“Is that my voice in your head when the bike slips?”

🦌

The water doesn’t remember us. But I do.
“Do you see my face?”

🦌

The light flickers. You hum. I forget the lyrics.
“Love in the roots. Love to go to.”

🦌

Maybe it is me. But you won’t like it.
So I let it go.

🦌
